# Rules of the Halloween Monster Game

## Core Idea
- Hidden roles / deduction
- Players choose actions each round
- Points gained/lost based on correct guesses and survival

## Monsters & Rewards
There exists different types of monsters with different rewards.
### Rewards
There are 3 types of rewards:
1. Life points: Those point correspond to the number of life points of the monsters. When a player kill a monster, he receives all the life point of this monster. they add up with his previous life points.
2. Special Weapon: Those weapon deals specific number of damages in specific ways
    - Double épée
    - Bombe
    - Poison
3. Winning Points
### Monsters
There are multiple type of monsters. They are all shown bellow:  
- Nain ou Gnome

- Géant
- Orque
