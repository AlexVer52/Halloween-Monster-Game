# Rules of the Halloween Monster Game

## Core Idea
- Hidden roles / deduction
- Players choose actions each round
- Points gained/lost based on correct guesses and survival

## Set up
At the start of the game, each players receive 2 things:
1. A basic weapon, the daguer, that deals 3 damage points
2. A specific number of health point (Ex. 5 or 8)

Players can then form alliances of up to 3 players (potentially to be adapted).These alliances are fixed and cannot be dissolved or expanded during the game. (There may be a health point modifier depending on whether you are in a team or not).

## Monsters & Rewards
There exists different types of monsters with different rewards.
### Rewards
There are 3 types of rewards:
1. Health points: Those point correspond to the number of health points of the monsters. When a player kill a monster, he receives all the life point of this monster. they add up with his previous health points.
2. Special Weapon: Those weapon deals specific number of damages in specific ways
    - Double épée
    - Bombe
    - Poison
3. Winning Points
### Monsters
There are multiple type of monsters. They are all shown bellow:
